Your GMCpay Account
Your GMCpay Account is an electronic money account which enables you to make electronic payments transfer. Your GMCpay Account is denominated in USD currency, therefore, the currency you transfer should be USD. Your GMCpay Account may be subject to charge, payment and withdrawal limits, depending on your country of residence, the verification status of your GMCpay Account and other factors used by us to determine such limits from time to time at our sole discretion.

You must take all reasonable steps to keep your GMCpay Account password safe at all times and never disclose it to anyone. Our personnel will never ask you to provide your password to us or to a third party. Any message you receive or website you visit that asks for your password, other than the GMCpay Website or a GMCpay payment gateway on a merchant website, should be reported to us. If you are in doubt whether a website is genuine, you should contact Customer Service. We also always advise you not to choose a password that is easily guessed from information someone might know or gather about you or a password that has a meaning. You must never allow anyone to access your GMCpay Account or watch you accessing your GMCpay Account. If you have any indication or suspicion of your GMCpay Account, login details, password or other security feature being lost, stolen, misappropriated, used without authorization or otherwise compromised, you are advised to change your password. You must contact Customer Service without undue delay on becoming aware of any loss, theft, misappropriation or unauthorized use of your GMCpay Account, login details, password or other security features. You must take all reasonable care to ensure that your e-mail account(s) are secure and only accessed by you, as your e-mail address may be used to reset passwords or to communicate with you about the security of your GMCpay Account. In case any of the e-mail addresses registered with your GMCpay Accounts are compromised, you should without undue delay after becoming aware of this contact Customer Service and also contact your e-mail service provider.
